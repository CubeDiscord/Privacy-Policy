Privacy Policy

Introduction

This privacy policy ("Policy") describes how Dimitar Iliev ("Owner") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you use the Cube server ("Cube") or its feature, Cube Connections ("Cube Connections").

About Cube

Cube is a Discord server that provides a platform for users to connect, chat, and engage in various activities. Cube is intended for users aged 16 and above. Users under 16 must have parental consent to use Cube.

About Cube Connections

Cube Connections is a feature within Cube that allows users to connect with other users who share similar interests. Cube Connections is strictly forbidden for users below 18. If you are under 18, you are not permitted to use Cube Connections. If the Owner discovers that you are under 18, the Owner will take appropriate action to address this violation of our privacy policy and terms of service. This may include suspending or banning your account from the Cube server. We are committed to protecting the privacy and safety of all our users, and we will not hesitate to take action against anyone who violates our policies.

Information We Collect

The Owner collects the following information from you when you use Cube:

  • User registration information:

    • Your Discord account's public information

  • Chat messages:

    • The Owner has access to all chat messages on Cube, but does not collect or store this information unless you report a text channel or the Owner has reason to believe that illegal or harmful activity is taking place.

The Owner collects the following information from you when you use Cube Connections:

  • Chat messages:
    • The Owner's bot may collect information about the content of your messages, including keywords and phrases within Cube Connections. This information is used to improve the Cube Connections feature and to detect and prevent prohibited conduct.

How We Use Your Information

The Owner uses your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with Cube:

    • The Owner uses your Discord public information (Discord username) to allow you to access and use Cube.

  • To protect Cube:

    • The Owner uses chat messages to detect and prevent prohibited conduct, such as harassment, bullying, or abuse.
    • The Owner uses chat messages to improve the functionality of Cube.

  • To provide you with Cube Connections:

    • The Owner uses your chat messages to connect you with other users who are also over 18 years old.
    • The Owner uses chat messages to detect and prevent prohibited conduct, such as harassment, bullying, or abuse.
    • The Owner uses chat messages to improve the functionality of Cube Connections.

Disclosure of Your Information

The Owner does not sell, rent, or otherwise disclose your personal information to third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law:

    • The Owner may disclose your information if required to do so by law, such as in response to a subpoena or court order.

  • To protect the Owner or others:

    • The Owner may disclose your information to protect the Owner or others from harm, such as in the event of a security breach or a suspected violation of Cube's Terms of Service or Cube Connections' Rules.

Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your personal information:

  • Accessing and correcting your information:

    • You can access and correct your information by contacting the Owner.

  • Opting out of Cube Connections:

    • You can opt out of Cube Connections by contacting the Owner.

  • Deleting your account:

    • You can delete your account by contacting the Owner.

Data Security

The Owner takes reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no data transmission over the internet is guaranteed to be secure, and the Owner cannot guarantee the security of your personal information.

Children

Cube is intended for users aged 16 and above. Users under 16 must have parental consent to use Cube. If the Owner discovers that you are using Cube without parental consent, the Owner may take steps to remove your account from Cube.

International Users

If you are located outside of the Republic of Bulgaria, your personal information may be transferred to and processed in the Republic of Bulgaria, where data protection laws may be different from the laws of your country. By using Cube or Cube Connections, you consent to the transfer of your information to the Republic of Bulgaria.

Changes to this Policy

The Owner may update this Policy from time to time. If the Owner makes any material changes to this Policy, the Owner will notify you by email or by posting the updated Policy on the Server or in Cube Connections.
