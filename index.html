Privacy Policy

Introduction

This privacy policy ("Policy") describes how Dimitar Iliev ("Owner") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you use the Cube Connections feature within the Cube server ("Cube").

About Cube Connections

Cube Connections is a feature within Cube that allows users to connect with other users who share similar interests. Cube Connections is strictly forbidden for users below 18. If you are under 18, you are not permitted to use Cube Connections. If the Owner discovers that you are under 18, appropriate action will be taken, including suspending or banning your account from the Cube server. We are committed to protecting the privacy and safety of all our users, and we will not hesitate to take action against anyone who violates our policies.

Information We Collect

When you use Cube Connections:

  1. Chat messages:
    • The Owner's bot may collect information about the content of your messages, including keywords and phrases within Cube Connections. This information is used to improve the Cube Connections feature and to detect and prevent prohibited conduct.

How We Use Your Information

For Cube Connections:

  • The Owner uses your chat messages to connect you with other users who are also over 18 years old.
  • The Owner uses chat messages to detect and prevent prohibited conduct, such as harassment, bullying, or abuse.
  • The Owner uses chat messages to improve the functionality of Cube Connections.

Disclosure of Your Information

The Owner does not sell, rent, or otherwise disclose your personal information to third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law: The Owner may disclose your information if required to do so by law, such as in response to a subpoena or court order.
  • To protect the Owner or others: The Owner may disclose your information to protect the Owner or others from harm, such as in the event of a security breach or a suspected violation of Cube Connections' Rules.

Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your personal information:

  • Opting out of Cube Connections: You can opt out of Cube Connections by contacting the Owner.

Data Security

The Owner takes reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no data transmission over the internet is guaranteed to be secure, and the Owner cannot guarantee the security of your personal information.

Children

Cube Connections is intended for users aged 18 and above. If the Owner discovers that you are using Cube Connections without meeting the age requirement, appropriate action will be taken, including suspending or banning your account from the Cube server.

International Users

If you are located outside of the Republic of Bulgaria, your personal information may be transferred to and processed in the Republic of Bulgaria, where data protection laws may be different from the laws of your country. By using Cube Connections, you consent to the transfer of your information to the Republic of Bulgaria.

Changes to this Policy

The Owner may update this Policy from time to time. If the Owner makes any material changes to this Policy, the Owner will notify you by email or by posting the updated Policy in Cube Connections.
